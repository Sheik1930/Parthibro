Our Love Story

First Meet

17 Sep 2014

The aroma of freshly brewed coffee, their eyes met over the rim of a cup, a serendipitous collision of souls. Time seemed to pause as fate whispered, "Here, here is where your journey together begins." And so, their love story unfolded, an enchanting melody composed by destiny itself.

First Date

30 May 2018

In a bustling street fair, she's laughter echoed, drawing him in. Among vibrant stalls and lively music, their laughter intertwined, weaving them closer. Amid flickering lights and the scent of summer, their hearts synchronized, marking the start of their journey together.

Proposal

02 Oct 2014

Beneath the golden hues of autumn, their love blossomed into a promise. With trembling hands and hearts full of hope, he knelt before her, offering a ring as radiant as their future together. As she said yes, the world seemed to hold its breath, celebrating the union of two souls destined to journey through life hand in hand, forever entwined in love's sweet embrace.

Enagagement

6 May 2024

Amidst blooming flowers and whispered promises of spring, their love takes another leap forward. With eyes filled with anticipation and hearts overflowing with joy, they exchange rings, sealing their commitment to a lifetime of shared dreams and endless adventures. May their love continue to grow brighter, illuminating the path ahead with the warmth of their unwavering bond.
